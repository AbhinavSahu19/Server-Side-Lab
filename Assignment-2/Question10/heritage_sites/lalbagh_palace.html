About Lal Bagh Palace

Lal Bagh Palace is one of the most majestic structures in Indore. Built between 1886 and 1921 by Maharaja Shivaji Rao Holkar, the palace boasts European architectural styles and opulent interiors.

History

The palace was used as a royal residence and hosted many prestigious events. Today, it serves as a museum, showcasing rare paintings, sculptures, and artifacts of the Holkar dynasty.
