About Kanch Mandir

Kanch Mandir, also known as the Temple of Glass, is a famous Jain temple in Indore. The temple is entirely made of glass and mirrors, creating a stunning visual effect.

History

The temple was built by Sir Seth Hukumchand Jain in the early 20th century. It is a magnificent example of intricate craftsmanship and religious art.
