About Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary

Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary is a serene natural habitat located on the outskirts of Indore. It is home to various species of flora and fauna, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers.

History

Established in 1981, the sanctuary covers an area of 5 sq km and serves as a protected area for native wildlife. It also offers trekking opportunities for visitors.
