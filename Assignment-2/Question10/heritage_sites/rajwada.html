About Rajwada

Rajwada is a historical palace located in the heart of Indore. Built by the Holkars of the Maratha Empire, this seven-story structure reflects a perfect blend of Mughal, Maratha, and French architectural styles. It is a symbol of Indore's rich cultural heritage and serves as a prominent tourist attraction.

History

Constructed in the 18th century, Rajwada has witnessed numerous historical events. Despite being destroyed and rebuilt multiple times, it still stands tall as a testament to Indore's glorious past.
