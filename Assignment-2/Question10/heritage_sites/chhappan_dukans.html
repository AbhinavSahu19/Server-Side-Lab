About Chhappan Dukans

Chhappan Dukans, meaning "56 Shops," is a renowned street food hub in Indore. It offers a variety of delicious local dishes including poha, jalebi, and kachori.

History

Chhappan Dukans has been a popular destination for food lovers for decades. The vibrant street remains bustling with locals and tourists throughout the day.
